A Letter for You, My Love

Three Years & Forever • 2026

My dearest Niki,

It feels like just yesterday when I first saw your smile, and yet I can't imagine a single day of my life without you in it. You've turned my world into something I never knew I needed: brighter, warmer, and full of meaning.

I still remember that quiet moment in the back of a taxi, when the city moved around us and everything else disappeared. That was when I knew. Not with words, not with grand gestures, just a feeling so certain it never left.

I love the way you laugh at my jokes even when they're not funny. I love that you see all of me, the parts I'm proud of and the parts I'm still working on, and you choose to stay anyway. You forgive easily, you love patiently, and somehow you make me feel like I'm worth every bit of that grace.

We've cried together too. Losing Onat left a hole that words couldn't fill, and when Sapi fell sick with the same thing, I held my breath and held your hand. But we got through it together, and watching Sapi healthy and thriving today feels like a small miracle we earned. Those were the moments I realized that having you isn't just about the good days. It's about knowing that even in the hardest ones, I'm not alone.

Every bouquet I've brought you was never really about the flowers. It was my way of saying: I still choose you. This month, and the month before, and every month I'm lucky enough to have. There's a version of me that existed before you, and then there's the person I am now, someone who loves deeper, dreams bigger, and believes in forever because of you.

I don't know what the next three years will bring. But I know I want to be there for every quiet morning, every laugh that catches you off guard, every hard night we get through together. More road trips, more lazy Sundays, more flowers, and more of your hand in mine.

Thank you for choosing me, every single day. Thank you for your patience, your warmth, and for making me feel like the luckiest person in the world.

You are my home. You are my always. I love you so much. ❤️

With all my love,
Yours forever,
Angkasa ✨
